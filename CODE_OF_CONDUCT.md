# Code of Conduct

This project should be a respectful and constructive space.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and patient.
- Criticize ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent where reasonable.
- Keep discussions focused and useful.
- Be especially careful in security discussions: clarity matters more than ego.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Deliberate intimidation.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Trolling or sustained off-topic disruption.
- Encouraging unsafe security practices.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or restrict participation when needed to keep the project healthy.
